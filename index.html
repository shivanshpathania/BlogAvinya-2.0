FAQS

How do I start a blog?

There are a few steps to starting a blog. You’ll need to choose a blogging platform, register a domain name, and start sharing your content.

Do I need a degree to blog successfully?

No, you do not need a degree to blog. I have seen bloggers with degrees in all sorts of fields, as well as bloggers without any formal education.

How do I get the confidence to blog?

This is a tough one. I think the key is to just start writing, and don’t worry too much about what other people will think.

What is the difference between a blog and a website?

A blog is a type of website that focuses on sharing information in the form of articles, or posts. Websites can be static, meaning they don’t regularly update their content. A blog, on the other hand, typically has new content added on a regular basis.

an online journal or informational website run by an individual, group, or corporation that offers regularly updated content (blog post) about a topic.

a frequently updated web page used for personal commentary or business content
